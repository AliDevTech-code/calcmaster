Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: January 2026

At CalcMaster, we believe that your financial data is your business alone. Our privacy-first engineering ensures that your information stays under your control.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, social security number, or address. Our calculation engines (Tax, Salary, ROI) are designed to process data locally in your browser. This means the values you input for calculations never reach our servers.

2. Local Processing

Our commitment to "Surgical Accuracy" includes a commitment to local security. Unlike traditional financial tools, CalcMaster's logic runs on your device. We use JavaScript for real-time results without storing your sensitive financial figures.

3. Cookies and Analytics

To improve our service, we may use minimal cookies or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand site traffic. These tools collect anonymous data such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited on CalcMaster
  • Time spent on specific calculators
  • Referring website URLs

4. Third-Party Services

We may display advertisements via Google AdSense or use cloud hosting through services like AWS. These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them as we do not control how they process anonymous browsing data.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our website. However, as a web-based suite, we recommend using a secure connection (HTTPS) whenever accessing our financial engines.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security of our tools, feel free to reach out to the ALi Developer engineering team through our Contact Page.
