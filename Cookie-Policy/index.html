Cookie Policy.

Updated: 2026 Stable Release

At CalcMaster, we use cookies to ensure our high-precision tools work smoothly on your device. This policy explains what cookies are and how we use them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser. They help us remember your preferences (like your last filing status or state selection) so you don't have to re-enter them every time.

How We Use Them

Cookie Type Purpose
Essential Required for core functionality like calculation processing and security.
Performance Helps us understand how visitors interact with our financial engines.
Advertising Used by partners like Google AdSense to show relevant financial ads.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages, such as Google Analytics and AdSense. We do not have direct access to the data collected by these third parties.

Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that some features of our Tax and Salary engines may not function with surgical precision if cookies are disabled.

For more details on data handling, please visit our Privacy Policy.
