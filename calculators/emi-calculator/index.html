Loan Structure.

In the United States, most personal and auto loans use a Fixed Rate Amortization schedule. This means your monthly payment stays constant while the principal-to-interest ratio shifts over time.

APR vs Interest Rate

APR reflects the true cost of borrowing, including interest and lender fees. Always calculate using APR for accuracy.

Impact of Tenure

While longer tenures reduce monthly payments, they significantly increase the total interest paid over the life of the loan.
