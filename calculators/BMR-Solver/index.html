The Science of Metabolism

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic life-sustaining functions, such as breathing, nutrient processing, and cell production. Even if you stay in bed all day, your body is hard at work using energy. Understanding your BMR is the first step toward effective weight management and long-term health.
